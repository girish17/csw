  4. object LOCAL extends TimeScale with Product with Serializable

    Local time, the date/time reported by your PC.

    Local time, the date/time reported by your PC. If your PC clock is accurate to a second then the other time scales displayed above will also be accurate to within one second.

  5. object TAI extends TimeScale with Product with Serializable

    TAI, Temps Atomique International, is the international atomic time scale based on a continuous counting of the SI second.

    TAI, Temps Atomique International, is the international atomic time scale based on a continuous counting of the SI second. TAI is currently ahead of UTC by 36 seconds. TAI is always ahead of GPS by 19 seconds.

  7. object UTC extends TimeScale with Product with Serializable

    UTC, Coordinated Universal Time, popularly known as GMT (Greenwich Mean Time), or Zulu time.

    UTC, Coordinated Universal Time, popularly known as GMT (Greenwich Mean Time), or Zulu time. Local time differs from UTC by the number of hours of your timezone.
